Project Overview

This e-portfolio presents a comprehensive analysis of The Stadthuys, Melaka's most iconic historical landmark. Through systematic evaluation using the Competitiveness Score framework, we examine this well-preserved Dutch colonial building's appeal, visitor experience, and potential for sustainable tourism development.

Our research methodology combines quantitative assessment across ten key indicators with qualitative insights gathered through on-site observation, visitor interactions, and multimedia documentation. This multi-faceted approach enables evidence-based recommendations that can enhance the attraction's competitiveness while preserving its authentic historical character.

Explore our findings through the navigation menu above, and discover how strategic improvements can transform tourist experiences while honoring The Stadthuys' significance as the oldest surviving Dutch building in the Orient.
